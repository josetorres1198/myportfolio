PAYCRAVE

UX & UI Design

Paycrave is a mobile app project assigned during my BLOC design apprenticeship. The app allows users to locate food trucks, order food and pay for their order prior to pick up. I created user flows, wireframes, design mockups and a prototype using Marvel for iOS 7.
